Online Accounting

  • All guides are available on our portal, accessible 24 hours a day.
  • You can create multiple users for your team, defining exactly what each can view.
  • Payroll events can be requested through the portal (e.g., hiring, termination, vacations, and absences) in a practical and secure manner.
  • Variable entries for payroll (e.g., absences, delays, overtime, etc.) can be made directly through the portal, eliminating the need for parallel controls.
  • Forgot to check a tax guide? Don't worry. Telles Assessoria will contact you to alert you before the due date.
  • We electronically capture all invoices directly from government servers.
  • We provide a free NF-e issuing software, which is much more practical and secure than the government-provided software.
  • We store all XMLs sent to Telles Assessoria for the legal period of 5 years, available for consultation on the portal from anywhere, 24 hours a day.
